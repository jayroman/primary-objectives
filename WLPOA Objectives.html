Governance & Board Operations

Community Spaces & Amenities

Reinstate Amenities Committee

Priority: High | Horizon: Short Term

  • Reestablish a dedicated committee to oversee community spaces and shared resources.
  • Ensure responsibilities are clearly defined and intentionally managed.
  • Provide structured oversight to maintain and enhance shared spaces.

Reinstate Watercraft Registration

Priority: High | Horizon: Short Term

  • Bring back a formal watercraft registration process to restore order and fairness.
  • To be managed by the reinstated Amenities Committee.
  • Prevent unregulated usage and protect shared amenities.
  • Enable accurate tracking and potential expansion of rack capacity.
  • Re-establish a known system while planning for future updates.

Priority: Medium | Horizon: Mid Term
